\section{\en Highest Common Factor \cy Factor Cyffredin Mwyaf}
\label{sec:highest-common-factor}

\en
The highest common factor (abbreviated to HCF) is the largest number that goes into two or more subject-numbers. For example, the common factors of 40 and 16 are 2, 4 and 8.
\cy
Y ffactor cyffredin mwyaf (wedi ei dalgrynnu i FfCM) yw'r rhif mwyaf sy'n mynd i mewn i ddau neu fwy o rifau. Er enghraifft, ffactorau cyffredin 40 ac 16 yw 2, 4 ac 8.
\en
The HCF of 40 and 16 is therefore 8. This is the largest number that `goes into' both numbers.
\cy
FfCM 40 ac 16 felly yw 8. Dyma'r rhif mwyaf sy'n `€˜mynd i mewn' i'r ddau rif.

\begin{example}
\en Find the HCF of 12 and 20.
\cy Canfydda ffactor cyffredin mwyaf 12 a 20.

\begin{solution}
\en The factors of 12 are: 
\cy Ffactorau 12 yw: 

1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12.

\en The factors of 20 are: 
\cy Ffactorau 20 yw: 

1, 2, 4, 5, 10, 20.

\en The HCF is therefore 4, as this is the largest number that goes into both 12 and 20.
\cy Y ffactor cyffredin mwyaf felly yw 4, gan mai dyma'r rhif mwyaf sy'n mynd i mewn i 12 a 20.

\end{solution}
\end{example}

\begin{example}
\en Find the HCF of 42 and 24.
\cy Canfydda ffactor cyffredin mwyaf 42 a 24.

\begin{solution}
\en The factors of 42 are 
\cy Ffactorau 42 yw 

1, 2, 3, 6, 7, 14, 21, 42.

\en The factors of 24 are 
\cy Ffactorau 24 yw 

1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24.

\en The HCF of 42 and 24 is therefore 6.
\cy Ffactor cyffredin mwyaf 42 a 24 felly yw 6.

\end{solution}
\end{example}

\begin{example}
\en Find the HCF of 12, 24, and 39.
\cy Canfydda ffactor cyffredin mwyaf 12, 24 a 39.

\begin{solution}
\en The factors of 12 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12.
\cy Ffactorau 12 yw 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12.

\en The factors of 24 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24.
\cy Ffactorau 24 yw 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24.

\en The factors of 39 are 1, 3, 13, 39.
\cy Ffactorau 39 yw 1, 3, 13, 39.

\en The HCF of 12, 24 and 39 is therefore 3.
\cy Ffactor cyffredin mwyaf 12, 24 a 39 felly yw 3.

\end{solution}
\end{example}
